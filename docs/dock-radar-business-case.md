# Dock Radar — Business Case
**Internal | March 2026**

---

## What is Dock Radar?

A proprietary signal intelligence tool that monitors LinkedIn and the web for companies actively deploying or selling DJI Dock-based drone infrastructure. It scores, ranks, and queues the highest-signal companies for BD action.

**Core hypothesis:** A company that publicly mentions DJI Dock = an operator or reseller building autonomous drone capability = a potential FlytBase partner or customer.

---

## Why This Matters Now

The dock-based drone market is forming fast. Most software vendors are waiting for inbound or relying on DJI's partner lists. We have a chance to build a proprietary pipeline of warm, signal-qualified leads before anyone else systematically does this.

The window is 6–12 months. After that, the market consolidates and these companies will have already chosen a software stack.

---

## The Three Business Outcomes

---

### 1. Partner-Led Revenue Pipeline

**The goal:** Convert high-signal companies into FlytBase DSP or SI partners.

**How it works:**
- Dock Radar detects companies mentioning DJI Dock across LinkedIn — ranked by signal strength (number of mentions, recency, context)
- AI scores each lead against relevance, use case, and geography
- BD reviews the queue each week and assigns outreach to the top tier

**Why it's better than cold outreach:**
The tool already knows *which post* triggered the signal. Outreach references the specific deployment — not a generic "we saw you use drones" message. Response rates are materially higher when the first line shows you've done your homework.

**First batch result (March 2026) — verified, FB partners excluded:**
- 151 DJI resellers scanned across 7 batches (B1–B7) + FP benchmark
- 56 companies showed DJI Dock signals · 12 excluded as existing FB partners (incl. Halo Robotics, DroneLand, GeoAerospace, Droprise)
- **44 net-new prospects**, never previously on FlytBase radar
- Signal breakdown: 7 high (5+ dock mentions) · 18 medium (2–4) · 19 low (1)
- Top prospects: TeamUAV (13 mentions) · Drone Parts Center (8) · AeroMind Poland (7) · ProFly Center DJI (7)
- Google News campaigns (C1/C2/C3) identified an additional ~40 companies across 24 countries — pipeline total across both sources: **~80 unique net-new companies**

**Target outcome:** 5–8 new partner conversations opened from each monthly batch.

---

### 2. Regional GTM Prioritization

**The goal:** Allocate BD resources based on where dock deployments are actually happening — not where we assume they are.

**How it works:**
- Every scored lead carries a country, city, and use case extracted by AI
- Analytics dashboard shows signal density by region and vertical
- BD and leadership use this to decide where to focus — which regions get campaigns, which get in-person visits, which get deprioritized

**Why this changes the decision:**
Most GTM plans are built on analyst reports or gut feel. We now have ground-truth data: real companies, real posts, real deployments. If 60% of dock signals are from Australia and the Middle East this quarter, that's where the pipeline will form — and we should be there first.

**Use cases beyond BD:**
- Product roadmap: which verticals are deploying dock right now? (Security, inspection, emergency response?)
- Investor narrative: proprietary market signal data as a differentiated asset
- DJI relationship: go to DJI's regional team with data on their own resellers, not just asks

**Target outcome:** Quarterly regional BD allocation tied to Dock Radar signal data, not assumptions.

---

### 3. Speed-to-Signal Outreach Advantage

**The goal:** Be the first software vendor to reach a company in the week they publicly signal dock deployment.

**The insight:**
The best time to sell FlytBase is when a company is actively building their dock capability — not six months later when they've already cobbled something together. LinkedIn posts about dock deployments are a real-time signal of that moment.

**How it works:**
- Dock Radar runs nightly, detecting new signals within 24 hours of posting
- High-signal new companies surface in the BD queue same morning
- BD rep sends a personalized outreach the same day — while the company is still in "build mode"

**What we're building toward:**
Auto-drafted outreach using the post content and company data as context. BD rep reviews, tweaks, and sends — no writing from scratch. The goal is signal-to-first-message in under 48 hours.

**Target outcome:** Average time from signal detection to first outreach < 48 hours. No competitor is operating at this speed today.
