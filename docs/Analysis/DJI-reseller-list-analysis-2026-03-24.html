1. What Was Built

A Puppeteer-based scraper that hits 6 different DJI "Where to Buy" API endpoints to discover every authorized DJI dealer globally. Data is stored in the dji_resellers Supabase table. A Phase 1 LinkedIn scanning script (Puppeteer + GPT-4o) classifies the most promising dealers by their DJI Dock deployment activity.

8. Key Insight for Leadership

The 547 High-Priority Dealers Are FlytBase's Warmest Prospecting List

These are companies that officially sell DJI enterprise hardware (Dock, Matrice) to commercial clients. They are either deploying DJI Dock for their customers (DSPs/SIs — direct FlytBase partner candidates) or reselling the hardware (channel partners who could bundle FlytBase software). Either way, they are the highest-intent audience for FlytBase's drone-in-a-box platform.

The Phase 2 LinkedIn scan will separate deployers (who actually install and operate Dock solutions) from pure resellers (who only sell the hardware). Deployers are the top-tier targets.

Constants & Classification Reference

All classification rules are maintained in src/lib/constants.ts:

  • OEM_NAMES — Hardware manufacturers (DJI, Skydio, Autel, Parrot). Excluded from partner pipeline.
  • Competitors — Drone-in-a-box platforms (Percepto, Azur Drones, Elistair, Asylon). Tracked for competitive intel.
  • DSP/SI Targets — Companies that OPERATE or DEPLOY drone solutions. Identified by activity codes + name keywords.
  • Buyers — End-users (police, utilities, hospitals). These are the DSPs' customers, not FlytBase partners.
