What is Dock Radar?

A multi-source signal intelligence tool
Dock Radar collects signals from 7 data sources — bulk databases, on-demand search, scheduled scans, AI browsers, and team intel — for companies deploying or selling DJI Dock infrastructure. It scores, deduplicates, and merges signals into a single prioritized queue for BD action.

Core principle: A company found by multiple independent sources is almost certainly a real prospect. Multi-source corroboration is the strongest signal.

Why these sources complement each other
Bulk sources (DJI Resellers, Registries) give us the universe — who exists in the drone industry.
On-demand sources (Google Search, LinkedIn Deep-Dive) let us investigate specific countries or companies.
Monitoring sources (LinkedIn + News Scanner) catch new signals as they appear over time.
Team Intel captures what no tool can — human conversations and relationships.

A company found across multiple source types (e.g., Instadrone: DJI dealer + LinkedIn post + French registry + Google result) is the highest-confidence prospect.

2 Score — Unified 0-100, Three Levels

Every source produces a raw signal. Before merging, each signal is normalized to a standard 0-100 scale and classified into three confidence levels.

One scoring language across all sources
Regardless of whether a company was found via Google, LinkedIn, or a government registry — the confidence output is always: HIGH MEDIUM LOW
Level Score Range Definition
HIGH 75-100 Company almost certainly deploys or sells DJI Dock. Confirmed deployment, named contract, or verified enterprise dealer.
MEDIUM 50-74 Strong signals but not fully confirmed. Multiple keyword matches, enterprise context, or BVLOS capability mentioned.
LOW 25-49 Mentioned in context but needs verification. Single keyword match, indirect reference, or unconfirmed operator.

3 Merge — Single Company Record

All sources write to one table: discovered_companies. When multiple sources find the same company, they merge into a single row with stacked evidence.

Multi-source corroboration = highest signal
A company found by 3 independent sources (e.g., Google + LinkedIn + DJI dealer list) is almost certainly a real prospect — regardless of individual scores.
