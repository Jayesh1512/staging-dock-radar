Gap Analysis & Known Limitations

1. Source Coverage Gaps
  • DJI Dealer list covers Enterprise dealers only — Professional, Retail, Agriculture, and Authorized dealers are excluded. Some may also sell Dock.
  • Google Search limited to 7 pages (~70 results) — Companies ranking on page 8+ or using non-English/Dutch/French Dock keywords are missed. Rivolier, Dumont, Geocentrum were found by Serper but NOT by Google Search.
  • Comet data is curated, not exhaustive — Only covers companies with explicit DJI Dock authorization or known involvement. Newer entrants may be missing.
  • ChatGPT Deep Research is point-in-time — Based on publicly indexed articles as of search date. Private deployments (like Shell/Skeye) may have more partners not yet public.
  • Serper site-search depends on Google indexing — If a company has Dock on a JS-rendered page (like Instadrone's hero slider) or behind authentication, Serper may miss it. Instadrone was initially missed for this reason.
2. Companies Likely Missing from This Report
  • Heliguy (UK/NL) — Appeared in multiple Google results as a Dock reseller + case study publisher. Not included because they're UK-based, but they serve NL clients (Shell case study).
  • Azur Drones (FR) — Major French autonomous drone company (Skeyetech platform). Competitor to DJI Dock but may also resell/integrate Dock. Not found in any source.

  • Non-dealer integrators — Companies buying Dock through dealers and deploying for clients (security firms, construction companies) won't appear in DJI dealer list or Google "DJI Dock" searches.

3. Confidence Caveats

  • "Dock 1, 2, 3" doesn't mean all deployed — For dealers, it means they sell all three. For operators, it means they've used multiple generations.
